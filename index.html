Conversation Guide
RAP
Review · Apply · Pray
R
Review
What did God say?
"What did God say to you this week?"
Let them share what stood out from Scripture or life. Listen more than you speak. Ask follow-up questions to draw them out.
A
Apply
How does it connect?
"How does that intersect with your life right now?"
Help them see God's Word as practical and personal — not just information. Bridge Scripture to their real circumstances.
P
Pray
Pray about the application
"Let's pray together about what you just shared."
Pray specifically about their application — not a generic list of requests. Model conversational, faith-filled prayer.
Things to Remember
Keep it simple

What is Baptism?
Baptism is a public declaration of following Jesus. Going under water symbolizes Jesus' death and burial; being raised out is a picture of dying to our old lives and being brought into new life with Him.

God still takes great joy when His children are baptized — just as He did when Jesus was baptized in Matthew 3:13-17.
